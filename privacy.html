Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 18, 2026

Soon is built to respect your privacy completely. This policy explains what Soon does — and, more importantly, what it does not do — with your information.

The short version: Soon collects no personal data. Everything you create stays on your device. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking.

Information we collect

None. Soon does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on any server. The countdowns you create — their titles, dates, themes, and icons — are saved only on your device and are never sent to us or anyone else.

No account required

Soon works fully offline. You do not create an account or sign in to use the app or any of its features.

Analytics and advertising

Soon contains no analytics SDKs, no advertising SDKs, and no third-party trackers. We do not profile you and we have no way to identify you.

Purchases

The optional one-time Pro unlock is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store using StoreKit. Soon never sees or stores your payment details. Your purchase is validated on your device against your Apple Account. Apple's handling of that transaction is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Sharing

If you choose to share a countdown card, the image is created on your device and shared only through the destination you pick (for example, Messages or another app). Soon does not upload it anywhere.

Children

Soon is rated 4+ and is safe for all ages. Because it collects no data, it collects no data from children either.

App Store privacy label

Soon's App Store privacy label is Data Not Collected.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
